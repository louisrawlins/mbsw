The Product

Eroe is an interactive online game produced in 2018. The storyline of the game is based off the classic Greek myths The Iliad and The Odyssey. This game is open to everyone that enjoys a good story or loves anything that has to do with Greek mythology.

The main character of the story is a hero by the name of Gluteus Maximillius. You get to play as Maximillius and make decisions that will decide your own fate. The storyline this game revolves around the choices the player decides which will lead Maximillius to victory or death.

This game is based on the Illiad, but we have added our own little twist to the story, including original characters with fun animations created and designed by us.

We hope that you enjoy the game.
