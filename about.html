The Product

Eroe is an interactive online game produced in 2018. The storyline of the game is based off the classic Greek myths The Iliad and The Odyssey. This game is open to everyone that enjoys a good story.

The main character of the story is a hero by the name of Gluteus Maximilius. You get to play as Gluteus and make decisions that will decide your own fate. We hope that you enjoy the game.
